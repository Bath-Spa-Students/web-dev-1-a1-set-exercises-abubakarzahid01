CHICKEN BIRYANI RECIPE

Description

Enjoy the tempting biryani recipe's spicy mixture of spices and delicate meat covered with fragrant basmati rice.

Each bite of this saffron, ginger, and clove-infused dish offers a taste experience inspired of Pakistan's vibrant streets.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (bone-in,skin-on)
  • Aromatics (ginger garlic paste)
  • Herbs (Cilantro, mint leaves)
  • Herbs (Cilantro, mint leaves)
  • Onions
  • Chili Peppers either raw green chilies or powdered red chilies
  • Ghee
  • Basmati rice

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in spices, herbs, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Separately prepare the fried onions.
  3. Boil rice in part with entire spices.
  4. Layer marinated chicken, fried onions, and rice in a pot.
    1. Marinate the chicken with yoghurt, spices, and lemon juice.
    2. Cook rice with entire spices until halfway cooked.
    3. In a pot, layer rice and chicken, then add fried onions and herbs in between.
    4. Cover and simmer over low heat until the rice and chicken are thoroughly cooked, resulting in a tasty biryani.
